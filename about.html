Our Story

Founded in 2010 by the Rossi family, Napoli Artesanal brings the authentic taste of Naples to pizza lovers around the world. Our journey began in a small kitchen in the heart of Naples, where Nonna Maria perfected her pizza recipe using techniques passed down through generations.

What started as a family passion project has grown into a beloved restaurant known for its commitment to traditional methods and exceptional quality. We continue to honor our Neapolitan roots while embracing innovation in our craft.

Our Mission & Values

At Napoli Artesanal, our mission is simple: to serve the most authentic, delicious Neapolitan pizza using time-honored techniques and the finest ingredients available.

We believe in:

  • Authenticity: Staying true to traditional Neapolitan pizza-making methods
  • Quality: Sourcing only the best ingredients, from San Marzano tomatoes to fresh buffalo mozzarella
  • Community: Creating a warm, welcoming environment where everyone feels like family
  • Sustainability: Supporting local producers and implementing eco-friendly practices
