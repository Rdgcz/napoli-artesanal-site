Our Heritage

Founded in 2010 by the Rossi family, Napoli Artesanal brings three generations of Neapolitan pizza-making tradition to authentic food lovers. Our story began in the heart of Naples, where Nonna Maria perfected her craft using techniques passed down through our family.

What started as a small neighborhood pizzeria has grown into a destination for those seeking the true taste of Naples. We remain family-owned and operated, with every pizza still made according to Nonna Maria's original recipes and strict quality standards.

Our Pizza Philosophy

At Napoli Artesanal, we believe pizza is not just food—it's an experience. Our mission is to serve the most authentic Neapolitan pizza using time-honored techniques that respect tradition while embracing quality.

Our core principles:

  • Authenticity First: Every pizza follows strict VPN (True Neapolitan Pizza) standards
  • Ingredient Excellence: We import key ingredients weekly from Campania, Italy
  • Craft Over Convenience: 48-hour dough fermentation for optimal flavor and digestibility
  • Community Connection: We're not just a restaurant—we're your neighborhood pizzeria

Why We're Different

  • Certified Neapolitan Craftsmanship

    We are one of the few pizzerias outside Italy certified by the Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana. This means every pizza meets strict standards for ingredients, preparation, and cooking methods.

  • Direct Italian Imports

    We source our "00" flour, San Marzano DOP tomatoes, and extra virgin olive oil directly from small producers in Campania. No compromises, no substitutions—just authentic Italian quality.

  • Wood-Fired Perfection

    Our custom-built Stefano Ferrara ovens reach 900°F, cooking each pizza in exactly 90 seconds. This creates the characteristic leopard-spot crust and smoky flavor that defines true Neapolitan pizza.
